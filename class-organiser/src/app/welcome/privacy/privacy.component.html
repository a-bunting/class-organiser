We do not use cookies on our website. Your privacy is important to us, and we aim to provide a cookie-free browsing experience.

We take the security of your information seriously. We implement industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data.
5. Data Sharing:
We will not sell, lease, or intentionally release any data provided to us to any third party. Your information is strictly confidential and will only be used for the intended educational purposes by those that first requested the data.
